Controlls for first person: 
W,A,S,D to move around 
E to interact
Escape to open the pause menu 

Minigame controlls: 
Hold left mouse to drag objects around the desk
Left mouse to interact with buttons
In cable mode left mouse on two objects will Add a cable connecting them useless there is already one present.
In cable mode right mouse on two objects will remove the cable connecting them. 
C can also be used to enter cable mode alongside the button.
